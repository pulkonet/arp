About

The only journey is the one within.

  • October 2017

    Our Beginning

    We started by selecting 1 TA for some selected course of each department just 3 week before end semester. It helped the first yearites to learn more effectively as they were able to connect more efficiently and confidently with the undergraduate students.

  • December 2017

    The Merger

    A new program formed. Having analyzed the need and feasibility of the programme and with an aim of not just restricting it to the first year, Institute Academic Council of SAC has proposed to merge UG TAship with Academic Reinforcement Program.
