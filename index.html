Vision

We envision to develop the knowledge base of the students, by abetting them to learn and teach, ergo in becoming active partakers in moulding the world to come.

Mission

Our goal is to administer a platform to apportionate knowledge with the entire campus by adept seniors therewithal giving them hands-on experience in becoming a faculty's teaching assistant and the opportunity to serve as a chaperone for the freshmen.

Initiatives

UGTAship

A knot of students are designated who will be acting as a bridge between the faculty coordinators and the freshmen as an Undergraduate Teaching Assistant (UGTA), for a selected set of courses. It opens the door for the students to get a hands-on experience in becoming a faculty’s teaching assistant and the chance to usher the freshmen.

Open Sessions

Open sessions would be instituted in the campus, wherein students from different fields can come up with presentations, talks or lectures on topics, they are inquisitive about. It would be a comprehensive platform for sharing knoweldge, open to all the students in the campus.

About UGTAship

Procedure

  • Eligibility: Any 2nd, 3rd or 4th year student can apply for the position of Teaching Assistant for a particular course, only if,
    • He/She has secured a grade of at least 8 in that course.
    • He/She does not have any active backlog.
  • Out of all the applicants, the names of few students for every course and for each branch will be selected on the basis of their communication skills
  • The selected students will be sent to the concerned faculty member with the understanding that the faculty member will choose the desired number of TAs on the basis of his/her knowledge of the subject.
  • If any TA is removed from his/her post due to poor performance, then new TAs will be selected.

Responsibilities

  • Each TA will be guiding at max 40 students for 1 subject of that branch.
  • Organizing lectures on the topic in which majority of students are facing doubts.
  • Clearing the doubts of the students in tutorial classes.
  • Clearing individual’s doubts either by suggesting important material or calling them in some common meeting place.
  • Liaise with the concern faculty
